About Me

I am fifth-year PhD student in Computer Science at ETH Zurich. I am part of DS3Lab and Systems group, where I am supervised by Professor Ce Zhang. I have a background in document analysis, applied computer vision, machine learning, and medical image processing. My current research focus lies in building systems for automatic understanding of semantic document structure in real-world documents (e.g. PDF files, scans). The goal of our research is to facilite large-scale document ingestion in machine learning and data analysis pipelines.
